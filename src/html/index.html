Trade Profit Calculator

for Star Citizen by Spraynpray

A prototype that features a customisable expression system to apply profit calculations to buy and sell activity in Star Citizen

Bear in mind this is a working prototype and trade isn't implemented in the alpha yet (due 3.0.0). Everything here is subject to change.
